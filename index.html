Pokemon Matching Game

How to Play

  1. You have 10 seconds to memorize the position of 28 randomly placed cards.

  2. After 10 seconds, all the cards will be flipped over.

  3. Your goal is to match as many cards as you can.

  4. However, each time you guess wrong, you lose a life.

  5. Don't guess randomly because you only have 3 lives.
